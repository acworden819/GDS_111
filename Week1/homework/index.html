I thought I would share a little bit about my favorite band. These are the first 3 albums, in order, released by the British band called Oasis.

These albums are considered by many to be their best albums, and a perfect triology of music. Here are all of their albums in their release order:

  1. Definitely Maybe
  2. (What's the Story) Morning Glory?
  3. Be Here Now
  4. Standing on the Shoulder of Giants
  5. Heathen Chemistry
  6. Don't Believe the Truth
  7. Dig Out Your Soul

The group was active from 1991 to 2009, however it was recently announced that they will be reuniting for a tour in 2025!

Oasis is my favorite band of all time, you can learn more about them on their official site here.
